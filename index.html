2016 Presidental Primary Race Heats Up

The 2016 election cycle is well underway as candidates fight to win their party's nomination by gaining the most delegates. Delegates are won at each state's individual primary or caucus, either at large or by a percentage of the vote, per state law. The crowded GOP field shurnk to just four hopefulls after several did not receive enough votes early on in the primary cycle while only Martin O'Malley dropped out of the Democratic field.
